# Privacy Policy — Jump!

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Summary

Jump! does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Period.

## Details

- **No data collection.** Jump! does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.
- **No network requests.** Jump! never communicates with any external server. It makes no HTTP requests, no API calls, and sends no telemetry.
- **No tracking.** Jump! contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party scripts.
- **Local storage only.** Your jump targets (shortcuts and URLs) are stored exclusively in Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync`. This data syncs across your signed-in Chrome browsers via your Google account — a standard Chrome feature that Jump! does not control or intercept.

## Permissions

Jump! requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Save and load your jump targets |

## Questions

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/bnutzer/jump/issues) on GitHub.
