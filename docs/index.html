Jump!

URL shortcuts at your fingertips. Navigate to your favorite sites instantly — from the address bar or with a single click.

Get Jump! for Chrome

Features

Everything you need for lightning-fast URL navigation, nothing you don't.

⌘

Omnibox

Type j followed by a space and a shortcut key to jump straight to any URL. For example, j g takes you to GitHub.

▣

Popup

Click the extension icon to see all your shortcuts as a clickable list. One click and you're there — no typing required.

⚙

Fully Customizable

Add, edit, delete, import, and export your shortcuts. Your targets sync across devices via Chrome's built-in sync.

See It in Action

A clean, focused interface that stays out of your way.

How It Works

Three steps to faster browsing.

1

Activate

Click the address bar or press Ctrl+L / Cmd+L, then type j and press Space.

2

Type a shortcut

Enter a shortcut key like g for GitHub or y for YouTube.

3

Jump!

Press Enter and you're there. No bookmarks to dig through, no URLs to remember.
